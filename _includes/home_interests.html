RESEARCH INTERESTS

What I work on

🌊

Natural Hazards

Flood risk characterization, compound flood event analysis, and hazard assessment in low-lying coastal and riverine systems.

🌡

Climate Change Impacts

Long-term trends in extreme rainfall, aerosol loading, and climate-driven shifts in energy demand and environmental conditions.

🌿

Nature-Based Solutions

Performance monitoring and modeling of green stormwater infrastructure — bioswales, rain gardens, and other low-impact development approaches.

🏠

Community Resilience

Machine-learning and multi-criteria methods for prioritizing flood adaptation strategies in vulnerable communities, with an emphasis on equity and local context.

📈

Hydrologic-Hydraulic Modeling

Field-calibrated stormwater and watershed models using PCSWMM and related tools, driven by corrected sensor data and design storm analysis.

🛰

Remote Sensing & GIS

Satellite-based analysis of vegetation recovery, atmospheric aerosols, and land-use change using Google Earth Engine and ArcGIS Pro.

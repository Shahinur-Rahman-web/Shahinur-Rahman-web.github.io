<div class="entry">
<div class="entry-head"><h3>Green Infrastructure Monitoring &amp; PCSWMM Modeling</h3><span class="entry-meta mono">Current PhD thesis chapter</span></div>
<div class="meta">Kreole Elementary School, Moss Point, MS · Tools: R, PCSWMM, ArcGIS Pro</div>

Field-calibrated hydrologic model of a bioswale and rain garden system at a Gulf Coast elementary school. I built a two-step R workflow to correct HOBO water-level logger readings for barometric pressure and timezone offsets, then used that corrected dataset to calibrate a 31-subcatchment PCSWMM model against NOAA Atlas 14 design storms.

</div>

<div class="entry">
<div class="entry-head"><h3>Compound Flood Driver Analysis</h3><span class="entry-meta mono">Analysis complete</span></div>
<div class="meta">Moss Point / Pascagoula River Basin, MS · 2008–2024 · Tools: R, NOAA, USGS</div>

Classified bivariate and multivariate compound flood events by combining daily rainfall records, USGS river discharge, and NOAA tide-gauge non-tidal residuals. Includes cross-correlation lag analysis and updated IDF curves for the region using Annual Maximum Series and GEV methods.

</div>

<div class="entry">
<div class="entry-head"><h3>Machine Learning for Flood Resilience Planning</h3><span class="entry-meta mono">In development</span></div>
<div class="meta">Moss Point, MS · Tools: R / Python, Random Forest, XGBoost, SHAP</div>

Random Forest and XGBoost models with Leave-One-Ward-Out spatial cross-validation and SHAP-based interpretation, used to prioritize nature-based flood mitigation options. Paired with MCDA/TOPSIS multi-criteria analysis and bootstrap uncertainty quantification.

</div>

<div class="entry">
<div class="entry-head"><h3>Air Conditioning Proliferation &amp; Cooling Policy in Bangladesh</h3><span class="entry-meta mono">Under major revision · Energy Research &amp; Social Science</span></div>
<div class="meta">Bangladesh · Tools: R, scenario modeling</div>

Scenario modeling of national air-conditioning demand growth under three pathways — business-as-usual (17% CAGR), moderate efficiency (12%), and accelerated policy with MEPS from 2026 (7%) — with projected implications for electricity demand and refrigerant emissions.

</div>

<div class="entry">
<div class="entry-head"><h3>Decoupling of Rainfall Totals &amp; Extreme Rainfall</h3><span class="entry-meta mono">In preparation</span></div>
<div class="meta">Bangladesh · Tools: CHIRPS, R</div>

CHIRPS-based analysis examining how total annual rainfall and extreme rainfall events are diverging across Bangladesh, with spatial and seasonal breakdowns of the divergence pattern.

</div>

<div class="entry">
<div class="entry-head"><h3>Long-Term Aerosol Optical Depth Trends</h3><span class="entry-meta mono">Analysis complete</span></div>
<div class="meta">Bangladesh · 25-year MODIS Terra record · Tools: Google Earth Engine, R</div>

Trend and change-point analysis of atmospheric aerosol optical depth over Bangladesh using Mann-Kendall tests, Sen's slope estimation, and PELT change-point detection, built on a Google Earth Engine workflow.

</div>

<div class="entry">

NDVI-based remote sensing analysis of vegetation recovery following large-scale population displacement and rapid land-use change in the Rohingya refugee settlement area of Bangladesh.
